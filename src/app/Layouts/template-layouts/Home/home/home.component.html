Milestones

ELLOUMI GROUP

COFICAB was founded in Tunisia in 1992 by Mr. Hichem Elloumi and is part of the ELLOUMI Group, the biggest Tunisian industrial and exporting group.

ELLOUMI Group specializes in various business activities, including automotive, power and telecom cables, wire harnesses, agribusiness, real estate, urban planning, retail, home appliances, consulting, and banking.

Founded in Tunisia in 1946 by Mr. Taoufik Elloumi, the ELLOUMI Group now includes 30 subsidiaries and employs over 10,000 people globally.

AN HOMAGE TO MR. MOHAMED TAOUFIK ELLOUMI

Mr. Elloumi (April 1921 - March 2005) founded the leading industrial and exporting group in Tunisia through his hard work and dedication. He established the core values of the Group: respect, equality, integrity, responsibility, dedication, and perseverance.
